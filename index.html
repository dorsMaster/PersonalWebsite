Activities

What I do!

When I am not busy with school, I take pictures and volunteer with different organizations! I do a lot of committee work, just to name a few, Engineering Faculty Council, Students' Council, Finance Commitee, and General Faculty Council. My major rules and responsibilities are named and explained below.

Peer Support

Throughout my High School Career, I was part of the Peer Support team where we prepared students in order to have enough resources to tackle both academic and emotional problems. Activities included presentations on how to handle stress and how to study in order to be more successful.

Engineering Students' Society

Been volunteering as the Academic Events Coordinator since September. March 2017, I was elected to be the Vice President Services. I ensure the creation and/or maintenance, timely procurement and delivery, and quality of ESS Services.

Swim Instructor

I was an Instructor for three years and worked with City of Edmonton, YMCA and the Scona Pool. Throughout my career, I taught many kids and learned how to communicate effectively. I have recently started teaching privately to my friends free of charge.

Iranian Students' Association of University of Alberta

A cultural group where through my position I promoted and represented ISAUA and acted as an ISAUA liaison to the SU and GSA in coordination with the President. Acted as a point of contact with the external organizations. As part of my reponsibilities I was also in charge of introducing new financial resources for the group.
